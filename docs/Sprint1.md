# AI in Control Engineering

---

## What is Control Engineering?

Control engineers design systems that:
- Monitor sensors
- Adjust outputs automatically
- Maintain stability and efficiency

Examples:
- Conveyor systems
- Temperature & pressure control
- Autonomous vehicles

---

## How AI Helps Control Engineers

AI enhances traditional control systems by:

- **Predictive Maintenance**
  - Detects failures before they happen
- **System Optimization**
  - Learns the best control parameters
- **Adaptive Control**
  - Adjusts behavior in real-time
- **Fault Detection**
  - Identifies abnormal system behavior

---

## Real-World Applications

- Smart factories
- Using AI in tracking systems

---

## Benefits of Using AI

- Increased efficiency
- Reduced downtime
- Improved safety
- Faster decision-making
- Lower operational costs

---

## Future of Control Engineering with AI

AI will allow systems to:
- Self-tune controllers
- Learn from data
- Make autonomous decisions
- Reduce human error
